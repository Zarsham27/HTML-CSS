HOW THE BUSINESS WAS FOUNED?

Leather Tree London was founded in a Class Room by Zarsham Haider with a aim to bring Luxurios and Stylish Leather Goods into the market with the help of his Long running Family Business


FOUNDER OF THE BUSINESS

Founder Zarsham Haider is a Fashion Student At Middlesex University London So he is really particular about the procduct which he launches into the market. He was born in 1999 in Kolkata, INDIA where he have grown watching manufaturing of Leather Product. He is the Third Generation in his family to Step Into the Leather Accessories Industy, Starting his First retail Shop in East London


WHAT WE SELL

Leather Tree London Sells a Large Line of Leather Product for Daily Use, Which Included Jacket, Shoes, Belts, Card Holder, Wallets, Bags, Hand Bags, Phone Case, Others
